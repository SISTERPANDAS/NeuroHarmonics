Mental Wellness Tips

Science-backed strategies to optimize your brain health.

FOCUS

The Pomodoro Technique

Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. This keeps your brain fresh and focused.

STRESS RELIEF

Binaural Beats

Listen to 40Hz binaural beats to naturally induce a state of concentration and calm.

MORNING ROUTINE

Natural Light Exposure

Get 10 minutes of sunlight within an hour of waking up to regulate your circadian rhythm.

SLEEP HYGIENE

Digital Sunset

Turn off all screens 1 hour before bed to improve melatonin production.

MINDFULNESS

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4s, hold for 4s, exhale for 4s, hold for 4s. Repeat 4 times.

POSITIVITY

Gratitude Journaling

Write down 3 things you are grateful for every morning to boost mood.
